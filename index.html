  • Looping Video: I can produce a looping video and livestream it on YouTube, and I’m happy to take questions during the session.
  • Support Ticket Submission: Ultimately, providing customers with a straightforward way to contact Support and submit a ticket will ensure we’ve done our utmost to facilitate this transition.

    CalDOJ SFTP Due date: 3/31/2025

    Objective:

    The objective of this project is to prepare Fulcrum for potential future submissions to CalDOJ, should we secure a PD or Jain in California. Currently, Fulcrum has no customers requiring direct submissions to CalDOJ, as all California customers submit through G2S Inc. (Mark and John), our Peer Provider.

    Our primary focus is to ensure that FbF LiveScan is accurately configured to submit to G2S, not CalDOJ. This preparation will allow us to respond swiftly and effectively should the opportunity arise in the future. Additionally, this project aims to complete any outstanding tasks related to this process, as I have been requesting their completion for over a year.

    Steakholders:

    Dana Clark, Amit Sharma, Shail Singh

    Current Status:
    Provided by Dana

    A temporary solution was found by adding /pub to the paths, though this should not be necessary. Further testing will be conducted shortly by reinserting the NIST field names to determine if it resolves the issue.

    History:
    Provided by Dana

    Testing is currently underway for the SFTP connection for California transmissions to G2.

    An issue has been encountered with the transmitter, resulting in an error when attempting to retrieve the configuration file from the server. According to discussions with John, the folder structures on the SFTP side should match those on the FTP side. Despite entering the same values, the error persists.

    Efforts are being made to find a workaround or identify the root cause of the issue.
